Running Ghost

  1. Go to <your URL>/ghost/signup/
  2. Enter your user details

You should now be logged into your Admin control.

Configuring Ghost

Go to <your URL>/ghost/settings/.

Blog Settings (/general/)

These are the Blog specific settings

  1. Change your Blog Title
  2. Change your Blog Description
  3. Upload a Blog Logo, this will be displayed in Themes over your Blog name.
  4. Upload a Blog Cover, this will be displayed in Themes as a heading image.
  5. Click "Save".

This will update all your Blog settings, you can check these by visiting your Blog URL.

User Settings (/user/)

These are the settings that control your User profile.

  1. Click on the Display Image and upload a dashing photo of yourself.
  2. Click on "Change Cover" and upload your own cover image.
  3. Fill in Location, Website and Bio with information.
  4. Click "Save".

Using Ghost

Write a Post

  1. Go to <your URL>/ghost/editor/
  2. Enter a Post Title
  3. Enter your content using Markdown
  4. Save your Post as Draft by clicking the 'Save Draft' button, or press Ctrl / CMD + S.

Publish a Post

Once you have written your Post.

  1. Click the arrow next to "Save Draft"
  2. Select "Publish Now"

This will set the save state to 'Publish Now' and the button will turn Red, once you press this button the Post will be published. To return the Post to a 'Draft' state;

  1. return to the 'Save Menu'.
  2. Select "Unpublish".
  3. Click the "Unpublish" button.

To change the "Slug" or "Publish date" of a post once published;

  1. Click the 'Settings' icon.
  2. Proceed to update the information as required (this will be saved automatically when you naviagte away).

Delete a Post

  1. Navigate to the 'Settings' icon in either the 'Content' page or 'Editor' page.
  2. Click "Delete".
